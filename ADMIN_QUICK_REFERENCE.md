## Photo Review Standards

### ✅ Approve If:
- Clear, well-lit photo
- High quality/resolution
- Meets type requirements
- Recent photo
- Only applicant in photo

### ❌ Reject If:
- Blurry or dark
- Low quality
- Doesn't meet type requirements
- Outdated photo
- Multiple people in photo
- Heavy filters/editing

## Photo Types

**Selfie:**
- Clear face photo
- No sunglasses
- No face coverings
- Good lighting

**Full Body:**
- Head to toe visible
- No baggy clothes
- Clear body shape
- Good lighting

**Activity:**
- Shows activities/hobbies
- Clear, high quality
- Only applicant
- Variety preferred

## Badge Verification

### Basic Tier
- Student: Valid student ID
- Professional: Business card/LinkedIn
- Athlete: Team photo/competition

### Elite Tier
- Entrepreneur: Business registration
- Graduate: Diploma/transcript
- Certified: Professional certification

### Star Tier
- Executive: Company profile
- Author: Published book
- Award Winner: Certificate/photo

## Common Actions

### Approve User
1. Review all photos
2. Review all badges
3. Tap "Approve"
4. Confirm

### Reject User
1. Review application
2. Tap "Reject"
3. Enter specific reason
4. Confirm

### Approve Photo
1. View photo
2. Verify quality
3. Tap "Approve"

### Reject Photo
1. View photo
2. Identify issue
3. Tap "Reject"

## Rejection Reasons

### Good Examples:
- "Selfie is too dark, please submit well-lit photo"
- "Full body photo shows baggy clothing, please submit fitted clothing"
- "Activity photos include other people, please submit solo photos"
- "Student ID is not clearly visible, please submit clearer proof"

### Avoid:
- Generic reasons
- Subjective appearance comments
- Discriminatory language
- Vague feedback
